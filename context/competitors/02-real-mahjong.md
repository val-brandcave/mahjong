# Competitor Analysis: Real Mahjong

**Category:** Mobile App - Direct Competitor

**Last Updated:** October 26, 2024

## Overview

**Product Name:** Real Mahjong

**Platform:** Mobile (iOS/Android)

**Business Model:** Unknown (likely paid or freemium)

**Target Audience:** American Mahjong players

**Market Position:** Low awareness, poor UI reputation

**Our Assessment:** Low threat - poor execution

## Competitive Positioning

### Their Strengths
- **Exists:** In the market with established user base
- **American Mahjong Support:** Implements NML rules
- **Mobile Platform:** Native app experience

### Their Weaknesses
- **Bad UI:** Consistently described as having poor interface
- **Low Awareness:** Not frequently mentioned by instructors/players
- **Poor User Experience:** Similar issues to other competitors
- **Not Beginner-Focused:** Assumes player knowledge
- **Technical Execution:** Quality issues reported

### Why They're Vulnerable
1. **UI Problems:** Core product experience is poor
2. **Low Brand Awareness:** Not top-of-mind for players
3. **No Educational Focus:** Game-only, no teaching
4. **No Instructor Support:** Not recommended by teachers
5. **Quality Issues:** Users report bugs and problems
