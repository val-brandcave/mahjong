# Competitor Analysis: Mahjongo

**Category:** Mobile App - Direct Competitor

**Last Updated:** October 26, 2024

## Overview

**Product Name:** Mahjongo

**Platform:** Mobile (iOS/Android)

**Business Model:** Unknown (likely paid or freemium)

**Target Audience:** American Mahjong players

**Market Position:** Best graphics among competitors, still has UI issues

**Our Assessment:** Closest competitor - strong visuals, weak UX

## Competitive Positioning

### Their Strengths
- **Best Graphics:** Described as having the best visual design among competitors
- **Visual Polish:** Attention to aesthetics
- **Modern Look:** Contemporary design elements
- **Established Product:** Some market presence

### Their Weaknesses
- **UI Issues:** Despite good graphics, usability problems remain
- **Not Beginner-Focused:** Assumes player knowledge
- **No Learning Path:** Visual appeal without teaching
- **Complex Navigation:** Graphics don't compensate for poor UX flow
- **No Charleston Focus:** Doesn't address #1 beginner pain point

### Why They're Vulnerable
1. **Graphics ≠ UX:** Pretty but still confusing
2. **Style Over Substance:** Visual polish without usability
3. **No Teaching Component:** Assumes you know how to play
4. **Instructor Gap:** Not recommended for beginners
5. **Missing the Point:** Looks good but doesn't solve actual problems
