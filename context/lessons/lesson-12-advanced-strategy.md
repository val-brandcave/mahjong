## Topics Covered

### 1. Defensive Play
- Don't just chase your win - prevent others from winning
- Safe discards (tiles unlikely to help opponents)
- Dangerous discards (tiles that might let someone win)
- When to play defensively vs. aggressively

### 2. Reading Opponents
- Watch exposed tiles (what patterns do they suggest?)
- Track discards (what are they NOT throwing?)
- Identify likely hands opponents are playing
- Adjust your strategy based on opponent positions

### 3. Strategic Discarding ("Playing Dirty")
- Hold tiles opponents need
- Discard tiles that aren't useful to anyone
- Force opponents to discard tiles you need
- Control the flow of available tiles

### 4. Joker Strategy
- When to use Jokers immediately
- When to save Jokers for later
- Joker substitution tactics
- Multiple Joker management

### 5. Risk Assessment
- Is someone close to winning?
- Should you go for high-point hand (risk) or safe hand (reward)?
- When to rush vs. when to take time
- Calculating probability of getting needed tiles

### 6. Tournament Play
- Different strategies for competitive play
- Point maximization
- Defensive play importance increases
- Speed considerations
