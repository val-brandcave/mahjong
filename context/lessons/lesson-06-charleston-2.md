## Lesson Overview

Building on Pass Right mastery, this lesson adds the two remaining passes of the First Charleston. Since users already understand the passing concept, these new directions are introduced more quickly. The focus is on learning the pattern and practicing the complete sequence.

**Tone:** Confident, building on prior success, encouraging
**Approach:** Leverage existing knowledge, add new directions, practice full sequence
**Key Message:** "You already know how to pass! Now you're just learning two more directions. You've got this!"

---

## Topics Covered

### 1. The Full First Charleston Pattern
- Three passes in sequence: Right ‚Üí Across ‚Üí Left
- Each pass = 3 tiles
- All passes are mandatory
- This is called the "First Charleston"

### 2. Pass Across
- Pass 3 tiles to the player ACROSS from you
- Receive 3 tiles from the player ACROSS from you
- Opposite player (across the table)

### 3. Pass Left
- Pass 3 tiles to the player on your LEFT
- Receive 3 tiles from the player on your RIGHT
- Opposite of Pass Right

### 4. The Complete Sequence
- Do all three passes in order
- Cannot skip passes
- Each pass uses 3 tiles
- Pattern: Right ‚Üí Across ‚Üí Left (remember: "RAL")
