## Learning Objectives

By the end of this lesson, users will be able to:
1. Identify all tile types (Bamboo, Character, Dot, Winds, Dragons, Flowers, Jokers)
2. Recognize tile symbols and numbers (1-9)
3. Understand tile quantities (how many of each exist)
4. Use basic Mahjong tile terminology correctly
5. Distinguish between suits and honor tiles
6. Understand what Jokers are and how they work

---

## Lesson Overview

This lesson introduces users to all 152 tiles used in American Mahjong. It's foundational knowledge that everything else builds upon. The approach is visual, interactive, and focused on recognition rather than memorization. Users will explore tiles by category, practice identifying them, and gain confidence through hands-on exercises.

**Tone:** Educational but fun, encouraging exploration
**Approach:** Visual gallery, interactive sorting, gamified practice
**Key Message:** "There are 152 tiles, but they're organized in simple categories. Let's explore them!"

---

## Topics Covered

### 1. Overview of the 152 Tiles
- American Mahjong uses 152 tiles total
- Organized into clear categories
- Four identical sets of most tiles (4x each)
- Some special tiles (Flowers, Jokers)

### 2. The Three Suits (108 tiles)
Each suit has numbers 1-9, with 4 of each (36 tiles per suit):

**Bamboo (Bams):**
- Green bamboo stick designs
- Numbers 1-9
- 4 of each number = 36 Bamboo tiles

**Characters (Craks):**
- Chinese character designs
- Numbers 1-9
- 4 of each number = 36 Character tiles

**Dots (Dots):**
- Circular dot designs
- Numbers 1-9
- 4 of each number = 36 Dot tiles

### 3. Honor Tiles - Winds (16 tiles)
- **East Wind:** 4 tiles
- **South Wind:** 4 tiles
- **West Wind:** 4 tiles
- **North Wind:** 4 tiles
- Total: 16 Wind tiles

### 4. Honor Tiles - Dragons (12 tiles)
- **Red Dragon:** 4 tiles (often has red character)
- **Green Dragon:** 4 tiles (often has green character)
- **White Dragon/Soap:** 4 tiles (often blank or simple design)
- Total: 12 Dragon tiles

### 5. Flowers (8 tiles)
- 8 unique Flower tiles (each different)
- Usually numbered 1-8
- Beautiful floral or seasonal designs
- Used for bonus points (advanced concept)

### 6. Jokers (8 tiles)
- 8 Joker tiles (wild cards)
- Can substitute for any other tile
- Cannot be used for Flowers
- Very powerful and useful

**Total Breakdown:**
- Suits: 108 tiles (3 suits √ó 36)
- Winds: 16 tiles
- Dragons: 12 tiles
- Flowers: 8 tiles
- Jokers: 8 tiles
- **Grand Total: 152 tiles**

## Accessibility Considerations

### Visual
- High contrast tiles on backgrounds
- Tile labels always visible option (settings)
- Zoom capability for tile details
- Color-blind friendly design (don't rely solely on color)

### Audio
- Tile names spoken when tapped
- Audio descriptions of tile characteristics
- Sound effects for correct/incorrect (with visual equivalent)

### Motor
- Large, tappable tile areas
- Drag-drop alternative: tap source, tap destination
- No fast reflexes required (except optional speed round)
- Adjustable timer settings

### Cognitive
- One tile category at a time
- Repetition built into exercises
- Multiple ways to learn (visual, audio, kinesthetic)
- No punishment for mistakes - unlimited retries
