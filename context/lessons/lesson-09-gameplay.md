## Topics Covered

### 1. Turn Sequence
- **Draw** a tile from the wall
- **Discard** a tile you don't want
- Announce discard clearly
- Each turn: Draw ‚Üí Discard

### 2. Starting the Game
- East goes first (has 14 tiles already)
- East discards first (no draw)
- Then South draws and discards
- Continue counter-clockwise

### 3. Calling Tiles
- When someone discards, you can "CALL" if you need it
- Calling exposes your tiles (no longer concealed)
- Cannot call for concealed hands (hands marked with C)
- First person to call gets the tile

### 4. Turn Rotation
- Counter-clockwise: East ‚Üí South ‚Üí West ‚Üí North ‚Üí East...
- When someone calls, they take their turn immediately
- Then continue from that person

### 5. Dead Hands
- Too many or too few tiles = Dead hand (you're out)
- Cannot win rest of game if dead
- Be careful counting tiles!

### 6. Drawing from the Wall
- Draw from where wall was broken
- Continue along the wall
- When wall section ends, continue to next player's wall
