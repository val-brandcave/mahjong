6. Understand who pays the winner

---

## Topics Covered

### 1. How to Win
- Complete hand matches pattern on card
- Have exactly 14 tiles
- Shout "Mahjong!" when you win
- Can win on your draw OR by calling someone's discard

### 2. Declaring Mahjong
- Say "Mahjong!" clearly and loudly
- Show your tiles to all players
- Prove hand matches card pattern
- First person to call Mahjong wins (if multiple could win)

### 3. Validating the Win
- Display all 14 tiles
- Show which line on card you matched
- Verify concealed/exposed requirements met
- Other players check validity

### 4. Basic Point Calculation
- Card shows point value for each hand
- Winner receives points from other players
- Who pays depends on how you won:
  - **Self-draw:** Everyone pays
  - **Call to win:** Only discarder pays (or everyone depending on rules)
- Advanced scoring in tournaments (optional)

### 5. After Someone Wins
- Round ends
- Optional: Track points/money
- Deal new game (East rotates after certain wins)
- Start fresh with new Charleston
