# Challenge 5.1: Tile Twins

## Challenge Metadata

**Challenge ID:** TILE-001
**Category:** Tile Identification
**Difficulty:** Easy, Medium, Hard
**Duration:** 1-3 minutes
**Unlock:** Available from start
**Skills:** Tile memory, matching, pattern recognition

## Description

**Objective:** Classic memory matching game with Mahjong tiles - flip and match pairs.

**Why:** Builds tile familiarity and visual memory in a fun, casual way.

## Mechanics

1. Grid of face-down cards
2. Tap to flip 2 cards
3. Match identical tiles
4. Matched pairs stay revealed
5. Find all pairs to complete

## Difficulty Levels

**Easy:** 8 cards (4 pairs), simple tiles, 60 sec limit
**Medium:** 16 cards (8 pairs), mixed tiles, 90 sec limit
**Hard:** 24 cards (12 pairs), similar tiles, 120 sec limit

## Scoring

- Each pair found: +100
- Speed bonus (Easy under 30 sec): +300
- Speed bonus (Medium under 60 sec): +400
- Speed bonus (Hard under 90 sec): +500
- Fewer flips bonus: +50 per pair under optimal

**Max:** 2,000 points

## 3-Star Requirements

⭐ Complete puzzle
⭐⭐ Complete under time limit
⭐⭐⭐ Complete with minimal flips (under optimal)

**Optimal Flips:**
- Easy: 10 flips (4 pairs + 2 errors)
- Medium: 20 flips (8 pairs + 4 errors)
- Hard: 30 flips (12 pairs + 6 errors)
