# Challenge 5.2: Spot the Difference

## Challenge Metadata

**Challenge ID:** TILE-002
**Category:** Tile Identification
**Difficulty:** Easy, Medium, Hard
**Duration:** 2-3 minutes
**Unlock:** 20 stars required
**Skills:** Attention to detail, tile discrimination

## Description

**Objective:** Find the one different tile among 10 similar tiles.

**Why:** Trains attention to detail - critical for not confusing similar tiles (5 Bam vs 5 Crak).

## Mechanics

1. Display 10 tiles, 9 identical + 1 different
2. User taps the different one
3. 15 rounds
4. Time limit: 10 seconds per round

## Difficulty Levels

**Easy:** Very different tiles (5 Bam among 6 Bams)
**Medium:** Similar suits (5 Bam among 5 Craks)
**Hard:** Nearly identical (1 Bam vs 1 Dot, or similar characters)

## Scoring

- Correct spot: +100
- Speed bonus (under 5 sec): +50
- Perfect accuracy (15/15): +1,000
- No errors: +500

**Max:** 3,250 points

## 3-Star Requirements

⭐ 12/15 correct (80%)
⭐⭐ 14/15 correct (93%)
⭐⭐⭐ 15/15 correct (100%)
