# Challenge 4.2: Symbol Decoder

## Challenge Metadata

**Challenge ID:** CARD-002
**Category:** Card Reading
**Difficulty:** Easy, Medium, Hard
**Duration:** 3-4 minutes
**Unlock:** 20 stars required
**Skills:** Symbol interpretation, notation reading, card fluency

## Description

**Objective:** Translate card notation (FF DDDD 1111) into actual tiles.

**Why:** Understanding card symbols is essential for knowing what tiles you need.

## Mechanics

1. Show card line: "FF DDDD 1111 2222"
2. User selects actual tiles that match
3. Drag tiles from pool to match pattern
4. System validates
5. 5 lines per challenge

## Difficulty Levels

**Easy:** Simple symbols (FF, DD, NNNN)
**Medium:** Complex combinations with C/X
**Hard:** Ambiguous notations, tricky patterns

## Scoring

- All tiles correct: +200 per line
- Partial credit: +50 per correct tile
- Speed bonus (under 30 sec): +100
- Perfect round: +1,000

**Max:** 2,500 points

## 3-Star Requirements

⭐ 3/5 lines perfect
⭐⭐ 4/5 lines perfect
⭐⭐⭐ 5/5 lines perfect, average under 40 seconds
