# Challenge 8.1: What Do I Need?

## Challenge Metadata

**Challenge ID:** COMPLETION-001
**Category:** Completion Challenges
**Difficulty:** Easy, Medium, Hard
**Duration:** 3-4 minutes
**Unlock:** 20 stars required
**Skills:** Completion analysis, gap identification, winning awareness

## Description

**Objective:** Identify which tiles you need to complete your hand.

**Why:** Knowing what you need is crucial - you can't win if you don't know what to look for!

## Mechanics

1. Show your 13 tiles
2. Show target hand from card
3. User selects tiles needed to complete
4. System validates selections
5. 10 scenarios

## Difficulty Levels

**Easy:** 1 tile away from winning (obvious)
**Medium:** 2-3 tiles away
**Hard:** Multiple possible completion paths

## Scoring

- Correct tiles identified: +200 each
- All completion options found: +500
- Perfect round (10/10): +2,000

**Max:** 4,500 points

## 3-Star Requirements

⭐ 7/10 scenarios correct
⭐⭐ 9/10 scenarios correct
⭐⭐⭐ 10/10 scenarios correct + all paths found
