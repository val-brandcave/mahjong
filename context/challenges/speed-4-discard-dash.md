# Challenge 2.4: Discard Dash

## Challenge Metadata

**Challenge ID:** SPEED-004
**Category:** Speed Challenges
**Difficulty:** Easy, Medium, Hard
**Duration:** 3-4 minutes
**Unlock:** 20 stars required
**Skills:** Quick decisions, strategic thinking, discard selection

---

## Description

**Objective:** Make 20 quick discard decisions (10 seconds each).

**Why:** In real games, you need to decide what to discard quickly while others wait for their turn.

---

## Mechanics

1. Show your rack + chosen hand
2. 10-second timer starts
3. Select tile to discard
4. System evaluates: Optimal, Acceptable, or Poor
5. Brief feedback
6. Next scenario (new rack/hand)
7. Complete 20 scenarios

---

## Difficulty Levels

**Easy:**
- Obvious discards (singles far from pattern)
- Clear "worst" tile
- 10 seconds per decision

**Medium:**
- Multiple reasonable options
- Strategic considerations
- 8 seconds per decision

**Hard:**
- Tough strategic decisions
- Keep vs. discard dilemmas
- 6 seconds per decision

---

## Scoring

- Optimal discard: +100 points
- Acceptable discard: +50 points
- Poor discard: +0 points
- Speed bonus (under 5 sec): +50
- Perfect decisions (20/20 optimal): +2,000

**Max:** 5,000 points

---

## 3-Star Requirements

⭐ 14/20 acceptable or better (70%)
⭐⭐ 16/20 acceptable, 10/20 optimal
⭐⭐⭐ 18/20 optimal decisions
