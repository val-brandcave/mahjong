# Challenge 6.2: Safe or Risky?

## Challenge Metadata

**Challenge ID:** STRATEGY-002
**Category:** Strategic Decision
**Difficulty:** Easy, Medium, Hard
**Duration:** 3-4 minutes
**Unlock:** 50 stars required
**Skills:** Defensive play, risk assessment, discard safety

## Description

**Objective:** Identify safe vs. risky discards based on opponents' exposed tiles.

**Why:** Defensive play prevents giving opponents winning tiles - key to consistent success.

## Mechanics

1. Show opponents' exposed tiles
2. Show discard pile
3. Present 3 discard options from your rack
4. User identifies: Safe, Neutral, or Risky for each
5. System validates assessment
6. 10 scenarios

## Difficulty Levels

**Easy:** Obvious safe/risky tiles
**Medium:** Need to analyze exposed tiles carefully
**Hard:** Complex game states, multiple opponents close

## Scoring

- Correct assessment: +150
- Safest choice identified: +100
- Perfect round (10/10): +1,000
- No errors: +500

**Max:** 3,500 points

## 3-Star Requirements

⭐ 7/10 correct assessments
⭐⭐ 8/10 correct + safest identified
⭐⭐⭐ 10/10 correct with all safest choices
