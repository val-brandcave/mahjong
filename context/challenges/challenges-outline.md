# Mahjong App - Challenge Modes Outline

A comprehensive system of quick challenges to train users' "Mahjong Eye" - the ability to quickly recognize patterns, make strategic decisions, and improve gameplay skills.

---

## Challenge Overview

**Purpose:** Provide bite-sized, focused practice to develop specific Mahjong skills
**Duration:** 1-5 minutes per challenge
**Format:** Timed, scored, and replayable
**Progression:** Easy → Medium → Hard difficulty levels
**Gamification:** Stars, leaderboards, streaks, achievements

---

## Challenge Categories

### 1. Pattern Recognition Challenges
Train ability to quickly identify matching hands and patterns

### 2. Speed Challenges

### 3. Charleston Mastery Challenges

## CATEGORY 1: Pattern Recognition Challenges

### Challenge 1.1: "Which Hand Is This?"
**Description:** Given a completed hand of 14 tiles, identify which line on the card it matches

**Mechanics:**
- Show 14 tiles arranged in winning pattern
- User must find matching line on card
- Time limit: 30 seconds per hand
- 10 hands per session

**Difficulty Levels:**
- **Easy:** Common patterns, clear matches
- **Medium:** Less common patterns
- **Hard:** Obscure patterns, similar-looking hands

**Scoring:**
- Correct answer: +100 points
- Time bonus: +50 if answered in under 15 seconds
- Streak bonus: +25 per consecutive correct answer

**3-Star Requirements:**
- ⭐ Complete all 10 (70% accuracy)
- ⭐⭐ 80% accuracy + under 20 sec average
- ⭐⭐⭐ 90% accuracy + under 15 sec average

**Difficulty Levels:**

## Technical Considerations

### Data Tracking
- Challenge completion rates
- Average scores per challenge
- Time spent in challenges
- Drop-off points (where users quit)
- Difficulty balance (too hard/easy?)
- Most/least popular challenges

### Randomization
- Generate new scenarios for replayability
- Pull from tile/pattern databases
- Ensure variety in repeated attempts
- No memorization of specific puzzles

### Performance
- Fast loading times (<1 second)
- Smooth animations
- Responsive touch controls
- No lag during timed challenges
- Offline mode support

### Fairness
- Same difficulty across attempts
- Consistent scoring
- Level playing field for leaderboards
- Validated results (no cheating)
