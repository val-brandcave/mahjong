# Challenge 6.3: Call or Pass?

## Challenge Metadata

**Challenge ID:** STRATEGY-003
**Category:** Strategic Decision
**Difficulty:** Easy, Medium, Hard
**Duration:** 3-4 minutes
**Unlock:** 100 stars required
**Skills:** Calling strategy, exposure decisions, tactical thinking

## Description

**Objective:** Decide whether to call a discarded tile or pass.

**Why:** Calling has strategic implications - exposing your hand, revealing strategy, losing flexibility.

## Mechanics

1. Show your tiles + target hand
2. Someone discards a tile you CAN use
3. Decide: Call or Pass?
4. Consider:
   - Do you NEED to call?
   - Will exposing reveal too much?
   - Are you close enough to win?
5. System evaluates decision
6. 10 scenarios

## Difficulty Levels

**Easy:** Clear yes/no situations
**Medium:** Tactical considerations
**Hard:** Complex strategic tradeoffs
