# Challenge 3.1: Charleston Confidence Builder

## Challenge Metadata

**Challenge ID:** CHARLESTON-001
**Category:** Charleston Mastery
**Difficulty:** Easy, Medium, Hard
**Duration:** 3-5 minutes
**Unlock:** Available from start
**Skills:** Charleston practice, tile selection, confidence building

---

## Description

**Objective:** Practice individual Charleston passes (Right, Across, or Left) with helpful feedback.

**Why:** Master each pass type separately before doing the full sequence. Builds confidence through repetition.

---

## Mechanics

1. Choose pass type: Right, Across, or Left
2. 10 rounds of that specific pass
3. System suggests good tiles (optional)
4. Select 3 tiles and pass
5. Receive 3 tiles back
6. Feedback on selections
7. Repeat 10 times

---

## Difficulty Levels

**Easy:** Visual guides, suggestions shown automatically
**Medium:** Suggestions available on request (tap for hint)
**Hard:** No help, pure decision-making

---

## Scoring

- Good selection: +100 per round
- Optimal selection: +50 bonus
- All rounds optimal: +500
- No hints used (Medium): +200
- Perfect round (Hard): +1,000

**Max:** 2,500 points

---

## 3-Star Requirements

⭐ Complete 10 rounds
⭐⭐ 7/10 optimal selections
⭐⭐⭐ 9/10 optimal selections (Hard mode)
