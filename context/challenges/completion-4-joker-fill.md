# Challenge 8.4: Joker Fill-In

## Challenge Metadata

**Challenge ID:** COMPLETION-004
**Category:** Completion Challenges
**Difficulty:** Easy, Medium, Hard
**Duration:** 3-4 minutes
**Unlock:** 100 stars required
**Skills:** Joker optimization, gap filling, strategic completion

## Description

**Objective:** Determine optimal Joker placement to maximize completion chances or flexibility.

**Why:** Jokers are wild cards - using them optimally can mean the difference between winning and losing.

## Mechanics

1. Show tiles with gaps
2. You have 1-3 Jokers
3. Where should Jokers go?
   - Immediate completion?
   - Maximum flexibility?
   - Keep options open?
4. System evaluates placement
5. 6 complex scenarios

## Difficulty Levels

**Easy:** One obvious gap to fill
**Medium:** 2-3 good options, tradeoffs
**Hard:** Complex optimization puzzles, multiple hands possible

## Scoring

- Optimal placement: +400
- Flexibility analysis: +200
- Perfect strategy (6/6): +2,500

**Max:** 6,100 points

## 3-Star Requirements

⭐ 3/6 optimal
⭐⭐ 4/6 optimal with reasoning
⭐⭐⭐ 5/6 or 6/6 optimal placements
