# Challenge 3.3: Strategic Charleston

## Challenge Metadata

**Challenge ID:** CHARLESTON-003
**Category:** Charleston Mastery
**Difficulty:** Easy, Medium, Hard
**Duration:** 4-6 minutes
**Unlock:** 50 stars required
**Skills:** Strategic passing, hand development, goal-oriented play

---

## Description

**Objective:** Given a target hand to pursue, complete Charleston strategically to build toward that hand.

**Why:** Charleston isn't random - strategic passing keeps useful tiles and improves your position.

---

## Mechanics

1. System shows target hand (e.g., "Build toward Consecutive Runs")
2. Complete full Charleston (9 passes)
3. Make strategic tile selections
4. System evaluates final rack:
   - How close to target hand?
   - Did you keep useful tiles?
   - Did you pass wastefully?
5. Scoring based on progress toward goal

---

## Difficulty Levels

**Easy:** Obvious hand (clear tiles to keep)
**Medium:** Multiple possible hands
**Hard:** Ambiguous, tough strategic choices

---

## Scoring

- Tiles match target: +100 per matching tile
- Optimal passes: +500
- Ready to pursue hand: +1,000
- Strategic mastery: +2,000

**Max:** 4,500 points

---

## 3-Star Requirements

⭐ 8+ matching tiles after Charleston
⭐⭐ 10+ matching tiles, good position
⭐⭐⭐ 11+ matching tiles, optimal position
