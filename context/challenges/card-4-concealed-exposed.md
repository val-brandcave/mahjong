# Challenge 4.4: Concealed vs Exposed

## Challenge Metadata

**Challenge ID:** CARD-004
**Category:** Card Reading
**Difficulty:** Easy, Medium, Hard
**Duration:** 2-3 minutes
**Unlock:** 50 stars required
**Skills:** C/X identification, rule enforcement, win validation

## Description

**Objective:** Identify concealed (C), exposed (X), or either requirements quickly.

**Why:** Critical for valid wins - many beginners lose games due to C/X errors.

## Mechanics

1. Show 10 card lines
2. For each, identify requirement:
   - All Concealed (C)
   - All Exposed (X)
   - Mixed (some C, some X)
   - Either (no requirement)
3. Timed: 10 seconds per line
4. Score based on accuracy

## Difficulty Levels

**Easy:** Clear C and X markers, 15 seconds per line
**Medium:** Mixed requirements, 10 seconds per line
**Hard:** Complex patterns with multiple requirements, 8 seconds

## Scoring

- Correct identification: +100
- Speed bonus (under 7 sec): +50
- Perfect round (10/10): +500
- No errors: +500

**Max:** 2,000 points

## 3-Star Requirements

⭐ 7/10 correct
⭐⭐ 9/10 correct
⭐⭐⭐ 10/10 correct with speed bonuses
