# Challenge 2.2: Speed Sorter

## Challenge Metadata

**Challenge ID:** SPEED-002
**Category:** Speed Challenges
**Difficulty:** Easy, Medium, Hard
**Duration:** 30-60 seconds
**Unlock:** Available from start
**Skills:** Organization, tile recognition, efficiency

---

## Description

**Objective:** Organize 13 scrambled tiles in your rack as fast as possible.

**Why:** Organized racks help you spot patterns and make decisions quickly in real games.

---

## Mechanics

1. 13 tiles appear in random order
2. Timer starts
3. Drag tiles to organize:
   - Suits together (Bam, Crak, Dot)
   - Numbers ascending within suits
   - Dragons together
   - Winds together
   - Flowers/Jokers separate
4. Tap "Check" when organized
5. System validates organization
6. Score based on speed + accuracy

---

## Difficulty Levels

**Easy:** Clearly distinct tiles, 60-second limit
**Medium:** Mixed tiles, 45-second limit
**Hard:** Very similar tiles, 30-second limit

---

## Scoring

- Completion: +500 points
- Under 30 seconds: +300
- Under 20 seconds: +600
- Under 15 seconds: +1,000
- Perfect organization: +200

**Max:** 2,200 points

---

## 3-Star Requirements

⭐ Complete in under 45 seconds
⭐⭐ Complete in under 30 seconds
⭐⭐⭐ Complete in under 20 seconds with perfect organization

---

## Organization Template

`[Jokers] [Flowers] [Dragons] [Winds] [1-9 Bam] [1-9 Crak] [1-9 Dot]`
