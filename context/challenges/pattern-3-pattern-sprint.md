# Challenge 1.3: Pattern Match Sprint

## Challenge Metadata

**Challenge ID:** PATTERN-003
**Category:** Pattern Recognition
**Difficulty Levels:** Easy, Medium, Hard
**Duration:** 2-4 minutes
**Unlock:** 20 stars required
**Skills:** Speed matching, tile organization, pattern assembly

---

## Description

**Objective:** Match tiles to complete 5 card patterns as fast as possible.

**Why:** Trains quick pattern assembly and tile selection - essential for knowing what tiles you need.

---

## Mechanics

### Setup
- Card line displayed at top
- 20 random tiles shown below
- Drag tiles to match pattern
- 2-minute time limit for 5 patterns
- Wrong tiles bounce back

### Gameplay
1. Pattern appears (e.g., "FF 1111 2222")
2. Drag correct tiles from pool to slots
3. System validates each tile placement
4. Complete pattern → next pattern
5. Complete 5 patterns or time expires

---

## Difficulty Levels

**Easy:**
- Simple patterns (FF 1111 2222)
- Distinct tiles, easy to spot
- 30 seconds per pattern allowed

**Medium:**
- Complex patterns with C/X requirements
- Similar tiles in pool
- 24 seconds per pattern

**Hard:**
- Complex patterns, ambiguous tiles
- Many distractors in pool
- 20 seconds per pattern

---

## Scoring

- Pattern completed: +150 points
- Speed bonus (under 20 sec): +50
- Speed bonus (under 15 sec): +100
- Perfect round (5/5): +500 bonus
- Time remaining: +10 per second

**Max Score:** 1,875 points

---

## 3-Star Requirements

⭐ Complete 3/5 patterns
⭐⭐ Complete 5/5 patterns, average under 24 seconds
⭐⭐⭐ Complete 5/5 patterns, average under 18 seconds
