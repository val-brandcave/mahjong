# Challenge 8.2: One Away

## Challenge Metadata

**Challenge ID:** COMPLETION-002
**Category:** Completion Challenges
**Difficulty:** Easy, Medium, Hard
**Duration:** 2-3 minutes
**Unlock:** 20 stars required
**Skills:** Winning recognition, instant awareness, excitement management

## Description

**Objective:** Recognize when you're one tile from winning and identify that tile.

**Why:** Missing winning opportunities because you didn't recognize them is tragic - this prevents that!

## Mechanics

1. Show 13 tiles + card line
2. User must recognize: "I'm one tile away!"
3. Identify the winning tile
4. Shout "Mahjong!" when spotted
5. 10 scenarios, speed matters

## Difficulty Levels

**Easy:** Obvious patterns, clear wins
**Medium:** Less common hands
**Hard:** Complex patterns, quick recognition needed

## Scoring

- Correct identification: +300
- Speed bonus (under 5 sec): +200
- Perfect round (10/10): +3,000

**Max:** 8,000 points

## 3-Star Requirements

⭐ 7/10 identified
⭐⭐ 9/10 identified with speed bonuses
⭐⭐⭐ 10/10 perfect, average under 5 seconds
