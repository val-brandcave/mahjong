# Challenge 3.4: Charleston Under Pressure

## Challenge Metadata

**Challenge ID:** CHARLESTON-004
**Category:** Charleston Mastery
**Difficulty:** Easy, Medium, Hard
**Duration:** 1-3 minutes
**Unlock:** 50 stars required
**Skills:** Quick decisions, pressure performance, time management

---

## Description

**Objective:** Complete Charleston with strict time limits per pass - make decisions quickly but wisely.

**Why:** Real games have social pressure to move quickly. Practice performing under time constraints.

---

## Mechanics

1. Full Charleston (9 passes)
2. Each pass has time limit (depends on difficulty)
3. Must select 3 tiles before time expires
4. Time penalty if timeout
5. Complete all passes as fast as possible

---

## Difficulty Levels

**Easy:** 15 seconds per pass
**Medium:** 10 seconds per pass
**Hard:** 5 seconds per pass

---

## Scoring

- Completion: +1,000
- Time remaining: +50 per second saved
- No timeouts: +500
- Under 1 minute total (Hard): +2,000

**Max:** 5,000+ points

---

## 3-Star Requirements

⭐ Complete with no more than 2 timeouts
⭐⭐ Complete with no timeouts
⭐⭐⭐ Complete in under 1:30 (Medium) or 1:00 (Hard)
