# Positioning Statement

## Document Overview

This document defines the market positioning for the Mahjong learning app, including target audience, competitive differentiation, value proposition, and messaging framework.

**Last Updated:** October 26, 2024

## Core Positioning Statement

**For** women aged 40-70 who want to learn or improve their American Mahjong skills,

**[App Name]** is an interactive learning app

**That** teaches the National Mahjong League rules through bite-sized lessons and engaging challenges,

**Unlike** traditional in-person lessons or static video tutorials,

**Our app** provides personalized, self-paced learning with instant feedback, making the intimidating Charleston and complex rules easy to master in just minutes a day.

**Primary Persona: "Pickleball Mom" (Linda)**

  - Women aged 40-70

  - Active social lives, hobby-oriented

  - Values social connection and mental stimulation
  - Willing to invest in hobbies and self-improvement

## Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors

**1. MahJongg Made Easy (DVD/Digital)**
- Strengths: Established brand, comprehensive rules coverage
- Weaknesses: Passive learning, no interactivity, no practice
- Our Advantage: Interactive exercises with instant feedback

**2. In-Person Classes/Teachers**
- Strengths: Personal instruction, social learning, real-time Q&A
- Weaknesses: Expensive ($50-100), intimidating, time-consuming, Charleston takes 30+ min to teach
- Our Advantage: Self-paced, private, available 24/7, Charleston broken into 5-minute lessons

**3. YouTube Tutorials**
- Strengths: Free, visual demonstrations
- Weaknesses: Passive, no practice, no personalization, fragmented
- Our Advantage: Structured curriculum, hands-on practice, progress tracking

**4. Physical Books/Guides**
- Strengths: Comprehensive reference material
- Weaknesses: Static, overwhelming, no practice mechanism
- Our Advantage: Interactive, guided learning path, immediate feedback

### Indirect Competitors

**Brain Training Apps (Lumosity, Peak)**
- Compete for: Daily engagement, cognitive improvement time
- Our Edge: Practical skill with social application

**Social Games (Words with Friends, etc.)**
- Compete for: Casual gaming time, social connection
- Our Edge: Real-world skill that transfers to in-person play

**Other Hobby Apps**
- Compete for: Discretionary spending on self-improvement
- Our Edge: Unlocks social opportunities, not just solo hobby
